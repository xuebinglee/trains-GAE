Directed Weighted Graph Editor


How To Use

Add a Node: double click in the open space
Add an Edge: drag from one node to another
Move the Graph Layout: Ctrl-drag a node
Click a node or an edge to select it.

When a node is selected: Hit Delete to remove the node.
When an edge is selected: Hit Delete to remove the edge. Hit Up Arrow to increment its length by one. Hit Down Arrow to decrement its length by one.
